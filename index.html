Common Questions

Who is this class for? +
Anyone who wants to be prepared — nurses, MAs, dental assistants, EMTs, teachers, daycare workers, parents, coaches, or anyone whose employer requires BLS certification.
Is this AHA certified? +
Yes. We are an AHA Authorized Training Center. You receive an official American Heart Association BLS Provider Card upon successful completion.
How long does the class take? +
Typically 2.5–3 hours. You'll complete classroom instruction and hands-on skill stations. Plan for a full evening starting at 6:00 PM.
How long is the certification valid? +
Your AHA BLS Provider Card is valid for 2 years from the date of issuance. We also offer renewal/recertification classes.
When do I get my card? +
Same night. You walk out with your physical AHA certification card after passing the skills test. No waiting weeks for the mail.
